Services

Enables independent pharmacies to compete with big pharmacy chains.
Enable patient to have access to right drug at right time.

Delivery

Enable pharmacy to provide last mile same day delivery for their patients.

Automatic Refills

Enable pharmacies to refill and gain on repeat customers.

Digitalization of patient data
