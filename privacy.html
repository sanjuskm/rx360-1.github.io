Rx360 Patient Privacy Terms

Rx360 is committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of patient information. These patient privacy terms outline how we collect, use, and protect patient data on our Delivery as a Service (DAS) platform. By using our services, patients agree to the terms outlined below.

Information We Collect

  • Prescription Information: We collect prescription details provided by patients for the purpose of facilitating the delivery of medications.
  • Contact Information: Patients may be required to provide contact information for communication related to their prescription orders and delivery.

Use of Information

  • Delivery Facilitation: Patient information is used to facilitate the delivery of prescription medications from local pharmacies.
  • Communication: We may use patient contact information to send order confirmations, delivery updates, and other communications related to their prescription orders.

Data Security

  • Encryption: Patient data is transmitted and stored using industry-standard encryption protocols to ensure its security.
  • Access Control: Access to patient information is restricted to authorised personnel involved in the delivery process.

Third-Party Partners

  • Local Pharmacies: Patient information is shared with local pharmacies solely for the purpose of dispensing medications and facilitating the delivery process.
  • Service Providers: We may engage third-party service providers to assist with the delivery process, and they are required to adhere to our privacy standards.

Retention Period

Prescription Information: We retain patient prescription information for the duration necessary to fulfil the delivery process and comply with legal requirements.

Patient Rights

  • Access and Correction: Patients have the right to access and correct their personal information.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Patients may withdraw their consent for the collection and use of their information at any time.

Legal Compliance

Compliance: Rx360 complies with applicable data protection laws and regulations governing patient information.

Updates to Privacy Terms

Notification: Patients will be notified of any material changes to these privacy terms.
